Features of Bionic Pet Butler

1. Automated Feeding System:

Allows scheduled or on-demand feeding through an app, ensuring pets are fed at the right times, even when owners are not at home. Customizable portions to control diet and avoid overfeeding.

2. Water Dispenser:

Provides fresh water to pets automatically, maintaining hydration with regular refill reminders or auto-refill from a connected source. Some models may include a filtration system for purified water.

3. Smart Waste Management:

Integrated waste disposal that includes an automatic litter box cleaner for cats or a waste collection tray for dogs. Hands-free cleaning with reminders when it’s time to change or dispose of waste.

4. Activity Monitoring and Alerts:

Tracks pets' movements, eating, and drinking habits, sending real-time alerts or daily reports to owners via an app. Helps monitor the pet’s health and activity levels, alerting owners if irregularities are detected.

5. Remote Access and Control:

Mobile apps allow owners to control feeding, hydration, and waste management remotely from anywhere. Two-way communication lets owners interact with their pets through a speaker and camera system, ensuring pets aren’t lonely.

6. Customization and Scheduling:

Customizable settings to fit the specific needs of the pet’s routine (e.g., feeding times, activity reminders). Automatic scheduling based on preset preferences, including different modes for weekdays and weekends.

7. Pet Entertainment:

Comes with an interactive toy feature that can be remotely activated to keep pets engaged while owners are away. May include laser pointers or automated toys that move around, giving pets a fun distraction.

8. GPS Tracking (Optional Feature):

For outdoor pets, GPS tracking can be enabled to monitor their location, ensuring they are safe and within a set perimeter. Owners can receive alerts if the pet leaves a designated area.

9. Health Monitoring:

Some models may come with advanced health tracking capabilities, including sensors that monitor heart rate, temperature, and activity levels.
